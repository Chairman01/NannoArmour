What nano-technology actually does.

Asphalt shingles age because they dry out and lose the oils that keep them flexible and waterproof. Once that happens they crack, curl, and shed granules.

Nano Armor penetrates the shingle at a microscopic level and restores that lost protection, so the shingle behaves more like new, and keeps doing its job through the seasons.

 

Rejuvenate, or replace?

Replacing every shingle on a roof is a major expense. In many cases a roof that looks past its prime still has years of life left once the shingles are treated and protected. It is worth finding out before you spend on a full tear-off.
