Our story

Nano Armor is a premium roof rejuvenation and protection solution designed to extend the life of asphalt shingles while enhancing their appearance and performance. Based in Calgary, Alberta, we use advanced nano-technology to penetrate and restore aging shingles, helping protect roofs from harsh weather, UV exposure, moisture, and premature deterioration.

Our treatment revitalizes your roof's flexibility and water resistance, giving homeowners a cost-effective alternative to premature roof replacement. By restoring and protecting the shingles you already have, Nano Armor helps maximize roof lifespan, improve curb appeal, and reduce long-term maintenance costs.

Built for Canadian weather

Our solution is made for demanding conditions, and few are more demanding than an Alberta year. Nano Armor delivers durable protection against sun, rain, wind, snow, and temperature extremes, season after season.
